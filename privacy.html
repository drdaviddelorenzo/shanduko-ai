Privacy Policy

Shanduko AI | Last updated: June 5, 2026

1. What We Are

Shanduko AI is a free WhatsApp chatbot providing sexual and reproductive health (SRH) information to young people in Zimbabwe. It is not a medical service and does not replace professional healthcare advice.

2. Information We Collect

When you message Shanduko AI on WhatsApp, we receive your WhatsApp phone number and the content of your messages. This is necessary to provide responses.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver relevant health information responses
  • To maintain conversation context within your current session

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Storage

Conversation history is stored temporarily on a secure server to maintain context within a conversation. You can delete your conversation history at any time by sending the message reset to the bot.

5. Third-Party Services

Shanduko AI uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Meta WhatsApp Cloud API — for message delivery
  • Groq API — for AI response generation

These services have their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is shared beyond what is required to deliver the service.

6. Children

Shanduko AI is intended for users aged 15 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 15.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at tynashmasvikeni@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Shanduko AI constitutes acceptance of any changes.
