Shanduko AI — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026  |  Last updated: June 5, 2026

Plain language summary: Shanduko AI is a free WhatsApp chatbot that answers sexual and reproductive health questions for young people in Zimbabwe. We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with advertisers or third parties beyond what is required to operate the service. You can delete your conversation history at any time by sending the word reset.

1. Who We Are

Shanduko AI is a non-commercial WhatsApp-based sexual and reproductive health (SRH) information service designed for young people in Zimbabwe. It is operated by Cyprian Tinashe Masvikeni, an independent developer and public health advocate based in Harare, Zimbabwe.

Shanduko AI is not a medical service. It provides general health information and education only. For medical emergencies or clinical advice, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Contact: tynashmasvikeni@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

When you interact with Shanduko AI via WhatsApp, we may receive and process the following:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number — used solely to route responses back to you
  • The content of messages you send — used to generate relevant health information responses
  • Conversation history — stored temporarily to maintain context within your conversation session

We do not collect your name, location, age, gender, or any other demographic information unless you voluntarily share it in a message.

3. Why We Collect This Information

We collect and process the above information for the following purposes:

  • To generate and deliver accurate, contextually relevant health information in response to your queries
  • To maintain conversation continuity within a single session so the AI can understand follow-up questions
  • To improve the reliability and quality of responses over time

The legal basis for this processing is legitimate interest in providing a public health education service, and consent by virtue of you initiating contact with the service.

4. Sensitive Health Information

Shanduko AI operates in the domain of sexual and reproductive health, which involves sensitive personal information. We treat all health-related conversations with the highest level of discretion.

  • No conversation content is used for advertising or profiling
  • No conversation content is sold, licensed, or disclosed to any commercial entity
  • Conversations are not reviewed by human staff unless required for a verified safety concern (e.g., a user expressing intent to harm themselves)
  • Anonymised, aggregated patterns (e.g., most common health topics asked about) may be used for public health research, with no ability to identify individual users

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

Conversation history is retained on our secure server for up to 30 days to maintain context across sessions. After 30 days, conversation records are automatically deleted.

You may delete your conversation history at any time by sending the word reset to the chatbot. This will immediately erase your stored history.

6. Who We Share Data With

To operate the service, your messages pass through the following third-party infrastructure providers. Each is governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Meta Platforms Inc. (WhatsApp Cloud API) — delivers messages between you and our server. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy and WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Groq Inc. — processes the content of your messages to generate AI responses. Groq does not retain message content beyond the immediate request. Subject to Groq's Privacy Policy.
  • Oracle Cloud Infrastructure — hosts our server where conversation history is stored. Subject to Oracle's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your information with any other third parties, including advertisers, data brokers, governments, or law enforcement, except where required by applicable law.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Our server uses firewall rules to restrict unauthorised access
  • Access to server credentials is limited to authorised personnel only

No system is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected users as soon as reasonably possible.

8. Children and Minors

Shanduko AI is intended for users aged 15 and above. Given the nature of SRH information, parental guidance is recommended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used this service, please contact us and we will delete the relevant data.

9. Your Rights

Under the Zimbabwe Data Protection Act (2021) and applicable international frameworks, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request that your data be permanently erased (or send reset to delete immediately)
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Objection — object to processing of your data at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tynashmasvikeni@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies and Tracking

Shanduko AI operates via WhatsApp and does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or web analytics on this website or within the chatbot service.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will post a notice on this page with the updated effective date. Continued use of Shanduko AI after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of Zimbabwe, including the Data Protection Act (Chapter 11:22, 2021). Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of Zimbabwe.
